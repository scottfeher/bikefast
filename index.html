WHAT IS BIKEFAST?

Here at BikeFast our main goal is to help Bristol go green and help with peoples day-to-day travel. We believe that biking is the way forward, it helps you keep fit and we provide a service which means anyone in the city center have easily available travel.

The way our service works is you select your nearest BikeFast service using the map shown below, from there you can then reserve a bike and pay for one that you will then be able to go and use freely. Look below for more offers on our membership service.
